What We Do

Our mission is to work with our clients to positively impact the quality of life for each pet that we look after. We are always continually raising the standard of excellence for pet care and client service.

We believe every pet deserves a loving home and proper care. Through our platform we connect pet owners, adopters, and veterinarians to make that possible.

How it works

Simple steps to help your pet

PawPaw connects pet owners, adopters, and veterinarians in one place — fast, easy, and free.

  • ✔
    Report a lost or found pet

    Fill out a simple form and we'll help reunite pets with their owners.

  • ✔
    Book a vet appointment

    Find trusted vets near you and schedule in minutes.

  • ✔
    Adopt or give for adoption

    Browse pets looking for a home or list your own.

  • ✔
    Shop for your pet

    Food, accessories, clothing and supplements — all in one place.
